Warning: Obsolete Maxthon

Violentmonkey 2 requires Maxthon later than v4.0.3.5000, since earlier versions may fail in transfering data. Versions later than v4.0.5.2000 are recommended since the perfomance are getting much better and pages will not get stuck as before.

It is highly recommended that you visit Maxthon.com to get the latest Maxthon. However, if you insist on this version, please visit Violentmonkey support page for a proper download and more details.
